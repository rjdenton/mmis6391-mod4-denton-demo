About Denton's Pet Shop

At Denton's Pet Shop, we’re more than just a pet supply store—we’re a family of animal lovers dedicated to providing the best care for your furry, feathered, and scaly friends. Our mission is to create a one-stop shop where pets of all kinds can find quality products, compassionate grooming, and a welcoming community.

Our Story

Denton's Pet Shop opened its doors in Milledgeville, Georgia in 2010, born from a deep passion for animals and a desire to serve the local community. Over the years, we’ve grown to become one of the most trusted names for pet owners in the area. What started as a small family-run business has evolved into a full-service pet store offering everything from food and toys to grooming and health services.

Our commitment to the well-being of your pets drives everything we do. We believe that every animal deserves love, care, and quality products, and we strive to make sure our store reflects that belief.

What We Stand For

Quality Products

We carefully select only the best products for your pets, from nutritious food to safe, durable toys.

Customer Care

We treat our customers like family, and we’re always here to offer advice and support for your pet care needs.

Community Focus

We’re proud to be a part of the Milledgeville community and actively support local shelters and rescues.
