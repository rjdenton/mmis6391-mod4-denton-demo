Our Services

At Denton's Pet Shop, we offer a variety of services to ensure your furry friends receive the best care possible. From grooming to pet sitting, our experienced staff is here to help!

Grooming Services

Our grooming services include bathing, haircuts, nail trimming, and more! We prioritize the comfort and safety of your pets, providing a relaxing environment. Whether your pet needs a simple wash or a full grooming session, we’ve got you covered.

Pet Supplies

We carry a wide range of high-quality pet supplies, including food, toys, and accessories for dogs, cats, birds, and small animals. Our knowledgeable staff is always ready to help you find the best products tailored to your pet’s needs.

Pet Sitting & Boarding

Going out of town? Our reliable pet sitting and boarding services ensure your pets receive the attention and care they deserve while you’re away. Our facilities are safe, clean, and monitored to give you peace of mind.

Training Services

We offer training programs for dogs of all ages. Whether you’re looking for basic obedience training or advanced tricks, our experienced trainers use positive reinforcement methods to help your pets learn and thrive.
